My research focuses on federated learning, game theory, and privacy-preserving machine learning. I aim to bridge theoretical modeling and practical application, especially in federated unlearning and decentralized optimization.

Selected Projects

Utility-Enhanced Personalized Privacy Preservation

Developed Group Local Differential Privacy (GLDP) framework for Hierarchical Federated Learning (HFL), enhancing privacy without sacrificing model performance.

Federated Unlearning with Upcycling Noise

Introduced noise recycling strategies to support efficient client unlearning, preserving model utility under strict privacy constraints.

Strategic Signaling for Utility Control in Audit Games

Formulated dynamic signaling mechanisms to optimize player utility under auditing scenarios in adversarial settings.
